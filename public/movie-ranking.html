The Best Movies According to Karen

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Muppet Movie

This movie is the best showcase of the talents and creativiity of Jim Henson as well as his innovative ideas. The songs are really fun as well.

Back To The Future

This is nostalgic and fun. The characters are well done and the plot is very interesting. Michael J Fox is also very talented.

Many Rivers to Cross

This is an oldie but the plot is very fun and the acting is great.
